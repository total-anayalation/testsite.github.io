A little about me

👋 Hi, I'm Anaya

I'm a sophomore at UW–Madison studying CS, Data Science, and Education Policy. I'm fascinated by how technology shapes institutions — and who gets access to it.

🤖 AI Safety

I'm active in UW's AI Safety org. I think alignment is one of the most important problems of our generation, and I believe people with policy backgrounds need to be in that conversation.

🔗 The intersection

In CS I love ML. In data science I gravitate toward causal inference and fairness. In policy, I care about how evidence actually makes it into decisions. Put those together and you get someone who loves a good messy problem.

☕ Outside class

You'll find me at a coffee shop with a book, exploring Madison's lakes, or going too deep on a Wikipedia rabbit hole. I believe in warm conversations and the power of a good question.
